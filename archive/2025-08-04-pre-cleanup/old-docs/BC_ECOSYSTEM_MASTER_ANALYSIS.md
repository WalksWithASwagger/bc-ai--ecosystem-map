## 🎯 Executive Summary

British Columbia has emerged as a world-class technology hub, ranking among the top 5 technology ecosystems in North America. With over $6.5B in startup funding, $3.5B in major exits, and 7 unicorns, BC demonstrates exceptional innovation capacity across AI, life sciences, cleantech, and enterprise software.

**Scale & Impact**:

**Growth Trajectory**:
- **25% annual funding growth** (2019-2024)
- **30% YoY job creation** in technology sector

- **2-3 unicorns** created per year
