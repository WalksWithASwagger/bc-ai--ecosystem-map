## 💡 Lessons Learned

### What Worked
1. **Public Sources**: Company websites, press releases, news articles
2. **Government Data**: Canadian Coast Guard contracts, grant announcements
3. **Acquisition News**: Well-documented in tech media
4. **LinkedIn Employee Counts**: Generally accessible

### What Didn't Work
1. **Crunchbase**: Requires paid subscription for verification
2. **PitchBook**: Paywalled financial data
3. **LinkedIn Company Pages**: Some require login
4. **Private Company Revenue**: Rarely disclosed

## 🚀 Recommendations for Future Research

1. **Ocean Tech Companies**: Access Ocean Supercluster funding data

3. **Award Winners**: Deloitte Fast 50, CVCA deals

1. **Focus on Free Sources**: Avoid Crunchbase/PitchBook dependencies
2. **Batch Similar Companies**: Ocean tech, biotech, SaaS
3. **Time-Box Research**: 5-10 minutes per company max
4. **Use News Aggregators**: Google News, Techmeme
