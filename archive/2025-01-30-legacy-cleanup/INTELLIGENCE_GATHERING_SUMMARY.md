## 💡 Key Learnings

### What Works Best
✅ Ocean Supercluster announcements (public, detailed)
✅ Government grant databases (accessible)
✅ Company press releases (primary sources)
✅ LinkedIn employee counts (mostly public)
✅ Tech media coverage (TechCrunch, BetaKit)

### What's Challenging
❌ Private company revenue (rarely disclosed)
❌ Crunchbase/PitchBook (paywalled)
❌ Early-stage funding (often undisclosed)
❌ Valuation data (except at funding)
