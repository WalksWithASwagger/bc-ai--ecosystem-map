## 🔍 **RESEARCH METHODOLOGY**

### **Phase 1: Database Status Check**
- Query existing Notion database for each company
- Identify companies already present vs missing
- Assess data completeness for existing entries

### **Phase 2: Systematic Company Research**
- Website analysis and company information
- Funding history and financial metrics
- Key people and leadership teams
- Technology focus areas and AI applications
- Government tier classification
- Recent news and developments

### **Phase 3: Data Organization**
- Create category-specific markdown research files
- Standardize data format for Notion import
- Validate information accuracy and sources
- Prepare batch import JSON files

### **Phase 4: Database Enhancement**
- Import new companies to Notion database
- Enrich existing company profiles
- Update missing fields and properties
- Verify data integrity and completeness

## 📊 **RESEARCH PROPERTIES TO COLLECT**

For each company, research and document:

### **🏢 Basic Information**
- Company name and legal entity
- Founded year and incorporation details
- Headquarters location (city, province)
- Website URL and social media presence
- Company description and mission

### **💰 Financial Metrics**
- Total funding raised (all rounds)
- Latest funding round details
- Valuation (if available)
- Revenue estimates (if public)
- Employee count and growth

### **👥 Leadership & People**
- CEO and co-founders
- Key executives and leadership team
- Advisory board members
- Notable technical leaders

### **🎯 Technology & Focus**
- Primary AI focus areas
- Technology stack and capabilities
- Key products and services
- Target markets and customers
- Competitive advantages

### **🏛️ Government & Recognition**
- Government tier classification
- Awards and recognition
- Government partnerships or contracts
- Media coverage and press

### **📈 Growth & Metrics**
- Trending score and momentum
- Recent milestones and achievements
- Partnership announcements
- Customer acquisition highlights
