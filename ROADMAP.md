## Long-term Vision

The BC AI Ecosystem Atlas aims to become the definitive resource for understanding and engaging with British Columbia's artificial intelligence landscape. By providing comprehensive, accurate, and up-to-date information, the Atlas will:

1. **Foster Collaboration**: Connect organizations across the ecosystem
2. **Attract Investment**: Showcase BC's AI capabilities to potential investors
3. **Guide Policy**: Inform government decisions and support programs
4. **Develop Talent**: Help individuals navigate career opportunities
5. **Track Evolution**: Monitor the growth and transformation of BC's AI sector

Through continuous enhancement and expansion, the Atlas will serve as a critical infrastructure component for BC's technology ecosystem.
