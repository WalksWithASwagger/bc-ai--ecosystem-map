1. **SVG** (vector, scalable) - Preferred
2. **PNG** (high-res, transparent) - Standard
3. **JPG** (fallback for photos) - Acceptable

### **Size Requirements**:

- **Optimal**: 200x200px to 500x500px
- **Maximum**: 2MB file size for Notion compatibility

### **Quality Criteria**:
- ✅ Official brand assets preferred
- ✅ High resolution and clarity
- ✅ Transparent or white background
- ✅ Professional appearance
