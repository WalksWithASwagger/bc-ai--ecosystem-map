### Strategic Findings
Based on comprehensive analysis of 355+ BC AI organizations, this report identifies **critical market gaps**, **investment opportunities**, and **strategic development priorities** for positioning BC as a global AI leader.

### Key Insights
- **üèôÔ∏è Geographic Imbalance**: 79% concentration in Lower Mainland creates untapped regional potential
- **üí∞ Funding Valley of Death**: Gap between seed-stage startups and scale-up capital
- **üî¨ Research-Industry Disconnect**: Academic excellence not fully translating to commercial success
- **üåê Global Market Positioning**: Strong foundation but missing strategic international partnerships
- **üéØ Emerging Technology Gaps**: Underrepresentation in next-generation AI applications
