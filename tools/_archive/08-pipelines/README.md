## 🎯 **PIPELINE ARCHITECTURE OVERVIEW**

Your BC AI Ecosystem Atlas will evolve from a static database into a **living intelligence platform** that continuously discovers, validates, and enriches data from multiple sources.

### **Core Pipeline Philosophy**
1. **Continuous Discovery** - Never stop finding new opportunities
2. **Quality-First** - All data validated before entry

## 🔄 **PIPELINE CATEGORIES**

### **1. FUNDING INTELLIGENCE PIPELINE** 💰
**Purpose**: Track all funding activity, predict investment trends, identify hot sectors

**Data Sources**:
- BetaKit, TechCrunch, VentureBeat (existing)
- Crunchbase API (new)
- AngelList API (new)  
- Government funding databases (ISED, SR&ED)
- VC firm portfolio pages (automated monitoring)
- SEC filings and regulatory announcements

**Intelligence Collected**:
- Funding rounds (pre-seed to IPO)
- Investor relationships and patterns
- Valuation trends by sector
- Geographic funding distribution
- Deal velocity and timing patterns

### **2. COMPANY DISCOVERY PIPELINE** 🏢
**Purpose**: Find new companies before competitors, track emergence patterns

**Data Sources**:
- University TTO announcements (UBC, SFU, UVic)
- Accelerator/incubator demo days (Techstars, 500 Global, CDL)
- Patent filings (Canadian IP database)
- Business registration databases (BC Registry)
- LinkedIn company creation monitoring
- GitHub organization creation tracking

**Intelligence Collected**:
- Pre-launch companies
- Spin-off and subsidiary relationships
- Founding team backgrounds
- Technology stack analysis
- Market positioning signals

### **3. PRODUCT & TECHNOLOGY PIPELINE** 🔬
**Purpose**: Track product launches, technology trends, competitive moves

**Data Sources**:
- Product Hunt (daily monitoring)
- App store releases (iOS/Android)
- GitHub trending projects
- Technical blog monitoring (company engineering blogs)
- Conference speaker lists and abstracts
- Patent application monitoring

**Intelligence Collected**:
- Product launch timelines
- Technology adoption patterns
- Competitive feature analysis
- Technical talent movement
- Research & development indicators

### **4. MARKET INTELLIGENCE PIPELINE** 📊
**Purpose**: Understand market dynamics, competitive landscape, opportunity gaps

**Data Sources**:

- Conference/event monitoring

**Intelligence Collected**:
- Market size and growth projections

- Economic correlation analysis

### **5. RELATIONSHIP INTELLIGENCE PIPELINE** 🤝
**Purpose**: Map ecosystem connections, identify key influencers, track partnerships

**Data Sources**:

## ⚡ **PIPELINE AUTOMATION FRAMEWORK**

### **Scheduler Architecture**
```
├── Daily Pipelines (High-frequency data)
│   ├── Funding news monitoring
│   ├── Product launch detection  
│   └── Social media sentiment
│
├── Weekly Pipelines (Medium-frequency data)
│   ├── Company discovery sweeps
│   ├── Technology trend analysis
│   └── Competitive intelligence
│
├── Monthly Pipelines (Deep research)
│   ├── Market analysis reports
│   ├── Relationship mapping updates
│   └── Strategic opportunity identification
│
└── Triggered Pipelines (Event-driven)
    ├── Breaking news alerts
    ├── Funding round announcements
    └── Acquisition/merger notifications
```

### **Quality Assurance System**
- **Confidence Scoring** (0-100% for each data point)
- **Source Reliability Tracking** (credibility weights)
- **Cross-Validation** (multiple source confirmation)
- **Human Review Queues** (for high-impact discoveries)
- **Automated Fact-Checking** (against known databases)
