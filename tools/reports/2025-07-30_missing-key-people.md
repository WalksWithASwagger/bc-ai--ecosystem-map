## Key People Research Methodology

### Sources for Key People Information

1. **Company Website**: Check "About", "Team", "Leadership", or "Contact" pages
2. **LinkedIn**: Company page often lists key employees and leadership
3. **Crunchbase**: Provides founder and executive information
4. **BC Business Registry**: Can provide director information
5. **Press Releases**: Often mention founders and executives
6. **Industry News**: Articles about the company may mention leadership

### Data Format Guidelines

Format key people information consistently as:

```
First Last (Title), First Last (Title), First Last (Title)
```

Examples:
- `Jane Smith (CEO), John Doe (CTO), Alex Wong (COO)`
- `Dr. James Wilson (Founder & Chief Scientist), Sarah Chen (Managing Director)`

### Batch Processing Strategy

1. **Start with high-potential organizations**: Those with websites and LinkedIn profiles
2. **Process by category**: Research similar companies together to leverage industry knowledge
3. **Regional batches**: Process organizations by region to leverage local knowledge
4. **Update in batches**: Use the Notion API to update multiple organizations at once
