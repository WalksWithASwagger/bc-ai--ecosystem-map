## KEY FINDINGS & INSIGHTS

### 1. Data Quality Issues
- **50% False Positives**: Half the companies don't exist as described
- **Incorrect Dates**: Most "2025" companies are either older or non-existent
- **Geographic Errors**: Some companies aren't based in BC

### 2. Funding Landscape
- **Total Verified Funding**: ~$12M across all verified companies
- **Average Round Size**: $500K - $5M
- **Funding Types**: Mix of equity, grants, and government support

### 3. People & Talent
- **Total Employees**: ~1,000 across verified companies
- **Key Hubs**: Proto, Improving, INTERSOG driving employment
- **Talent Gap**: Heavy reliance on imported talent

### 4. Sector Distribution
- **Enterprise AI**: 40% (Proto, Silo AI, Denologix)
- **Cleantech/Sustainability**: 25% (Rockburst, Orca Water)
- **IT Services**: 25% (INTERSOG, Improving)
- **Community**: 10% (Vancouver AI Meetup)

### 5. Geographic Clusters
- **Downtown Vancouver**: Most companies
- **Burnaby/Richmond**: Some satellite offices
- **Remote**: Significant distributed teams
