### 🎯 CORE PRODUCT & TECHNOLOGY

#### AI Coaching Platform
Athletica AI is a sports science platform that analyzes performance and recovery to adjust each workout, keeping training on point. The platform adapts training based on workouts, with plans evolving as athletes train, guided by AI and sports science.

#### Key Technologies:
1. **Dynamic Load Optimization Algorithms**
   - Provides immediate feedback on player performance
   - Enables real-time adjustments to training intensity
   - Systems allow sports scientists to generate customized training plans

2. **Adaptive Training Intelligence**
   - Real-time adaptation based on actual athlete response
   - Not following one-size-fits-all training plans
   - Smarter sessions, fewer setbacks, no burnout

3. **Workout Wizard**
   - Advanced load optimizer ensuring optimal training sessions
   - Combined with unique AI-driven session design
