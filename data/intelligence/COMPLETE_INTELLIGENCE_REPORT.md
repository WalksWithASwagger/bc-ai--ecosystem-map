## 💡 Key Insights

### 1. Funding Patterns
- **Series B Sweet Spot**: $10-25M typical, $115M outlier (Aspect)
- **Seed Rounds**: $2-6M common for BC startups
- **Government Match**: Often 40-50% of project costs
- **Exit Multiples**: 10-20x on successful acquisitions

### 2. Sector Strengths
- **Ocean Tech**: Strong government support via Supercluster
- **Biotech/Life Sciences**: Major funding rounds (Aspect, Lila Sciences)
- **B2B SaaS**: Consistent seed funding (Browse AI, Produce8)
- **Robotics**: Successful exits (Kindred, Iris Automation)

### 3. Geographic Distribution
- **Vancouver**: 70% of funded companies
- **Victoria**: Strong in ocean tech and marine AI
- **Interior BC**: Limited but growing (Kelowna emerging)

### 4. Investor Patterns
- **Local Leaders**: OMERS, BDC Capital, InBC
- **US Participation**: Insight, Accel, Bessemer common
- **Strategic Buyers**: Microsoft, Ocado, Deloitte active
