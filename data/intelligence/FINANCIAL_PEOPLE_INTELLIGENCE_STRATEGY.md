# Financial & People Intelligence Strategy
*BC AI Ecosystem Atlas - Focused Enhancement Initiative*

## 🎯 Strategic Focus

Concentrating on the two highest-value intelligence categories:
1. **Financial Intelligence** - Funding, valuations, revenue, financial metrics
2. **People Intelligence** - Leadership, founders, key executives, board composition

## 💰 Financial Intelligence Priorities

### Tier 1: Critical Financial Data
- **Funding History**: All rounds, amounts, dates, lead investors
- **Valuation Tracking**: Pre/post money valuations at each round
- **Revenue Metrics**: ARR, growth rates, revenue multiples where disclosed
- **Financial Health**: Burn rate, runway, profitability status

### Tier 2: Advanced Financial Metrics
- **Investor Composition**: Board seats, ownership percentages
- **Follow-on Activity**: Insider participation, up-rounds vs down-rounds
- **Acquisition Multiples**: Revenue/ARR multiples on exits
- **Government Dilution**: Impact of grants on equity structure

### High-Value Financial Targets
1. **Hootsuite** - Revenue growth, path to IPO, latest valuation
2. **Aspect Biosystems** - Series B terms, runway from $115M
3. **Browse AI** - Post-seed metrics, Series A preparation
4. **Produce8** - Revenue traction, growth metrics
5. **Flytographer** - Revenue multiple analysis ($3.8M disclosed)

## 👥 People Intelligence Priorities

### Tier 1: Leadership Mapping
- **Founders**: Background, equity stakes, previous exits
- **C-Suite**: CEO, CTO, CFO profiles and compensation
- **Board Composition**: Investor directors, independent members
- **Key Technical Leaders**: VP Engineering, Head of AI/ML

### Tier 2: Strategic People Intel
- **Previous Company Success**: Track record of leadership team
- **Advisory Board**: Industry connections and expertise
- **Key Departures**: Founder/executive departures and impact
- **Hiring Velocity**: Key role additions, team scaling patterns

### High-Value People Targets
